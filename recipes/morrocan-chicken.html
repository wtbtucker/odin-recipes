Morrocan Chicken

Description

Create an easy and healthy meal on a weeknight using the Instant Pot. This recipe makes chicken thighs, sweet potatoes, and quinoa tied together by Morroco-inspired spices. If the downtime during each of the three dishes is used efficiently this recipe can be made and cleaned up in under an hour. This dish is an excellent meal-prep option because it reheats well.

Ingredients

Sweet Potatoes

  • 2 sweet potatoes
  • 1/2 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 tablespoon chili powder

Chicken

  • 1.5 lbs. chicken thighs
  • 1 tablespoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon ginger powder
  • 1.5 teaspoons garlic powder
  • 1/2 tablesppon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt
  • 1/3 cup raisins
  • 1/2 cup chopped green olives
  • 1/2 chicken broth
  • 1 coarsely chopped white onion
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil

Couscous

  • 1 cup water
  • 1 cup couscous
  • zest of one lemon
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/4 chopped fresh cilantro

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees Farenheit. Place sweet potatoes on baking sheet and drizzle with olive oil, garlic powder, and chili powder. Mix well. Bake for 20-25 minutes
  2. For the chicken, mix cumin, turmeric, ginger powder, garlic powder, chili, cayenne, and salt together. Rub chicken with spices making sure each thigh is thoroughly coated. Set aside
  3. Set Instant Pot to "Saute". When the Instant Pot is hot add two tablespoons of olive oil. When the oil is fragrant place chicken thighs in the pot. Cook for 2-3 minutes on each side then remove from Instant Pot.
  4. Place the remaining ingredients for the chicken in the instant pot and mix well. Place the chicken on top. Set the Instant Pot to Manual and High Pressure for 10 minutes.
  5. For the couscous, bring 1 cup of water to a boil. When water boils, remove from heat, add couscous, and cover. Let sit for 5 minutes. Uncover and fluff couscous with a fork
  6. Mix the remainin ingredients for the couscous in a large bowl with the cooked couscous
  7. When the Instant Pot is done cooking (indicated by a series of beeps) let the pressure release naturally until the pin on the lid depresses.
  8. Shred chicken with two forks. Serve with sweet potatoes and couscous
